Recipe: Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish is made with simple ingredients and bursts with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the spaghetti until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, cook the pancetta or guanciale until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Pecorino Romano, Parmesan, minced garlic, and a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the pancetta, then pour the egg and cheese mixture over it. Quickly toss to combine and coat the pasta evenly.
  5. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and extra cheese if desired.
